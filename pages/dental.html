Dental FAQs

Is sedation safe for my pet?

Yes. We use minimal, carefully monitored sedation for professional cleanings. Pre-anesthetic bloodwork is always done first to ensure safety.

How often should my pet's teeth be cleaned?

Most dogs and cats benefit from a professional cleaning once a year, combined with at-home brushing 3–4 times per week.

My pet hates having their mouth touched. Can you still help?

Absolutely. We use gentle desensitization techniques and can use very light sedation to ensure a stress-free experience for anxious pets.
